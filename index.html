About Me

I received B.Eng. degree from School of Computer Science and Engineering in Sun Yat-sen University, and received M.Sc degree from School of Artificial Intelligence in Nanjing University, under the supervision of Associate Prof. Chao Qian, and a member of LAMDA Group, led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.
